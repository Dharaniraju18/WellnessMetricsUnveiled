About Us

Wellness Metrics Unveiled is a hospital analytics dashboard developed using collected healthcare data. It offers a comprehensive view into hospital performance, billing, patient insights, and departmental efficiency. The system empowers users to explore key healthcare trends through interactive visuals, aiding informed decision-making.

Overview

  • 📋 View patient metrics from collected data
  • 📊 Analyze hospital performance trends
  • 💉 Monitor critical care efficiency
  • 💪 Track recovery rates across departments

Features

Key Functionalities

  • 📈 Visualization of total bill amounts and insurance coverage distribution
  • 🧾 Breakdown of out-of-pocket costs by patient and state
  • 🧑‍⚕️ Analysis of nurse performance based on medications and billing
  • 📍 Geographical representation of heart rate and billing by state
  • 🧪 Symptom-based lab test ordering patterns
  • 📊 Gender-wise insurance coverage and department-wise expense tracking
